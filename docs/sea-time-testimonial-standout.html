Making the Testimonial Pack stand out. Four ways to lift it off the page of white cards — the captain-signed pack is the “deliverable”, so it can earn more weight. Each shows the header + a slice of step 1.

A

Navy header band

Deep-navy header strip (cream title, terracotta eyebrow); body stays white. Premium and editorial, low ink.
