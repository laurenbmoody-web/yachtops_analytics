Rest panel — more options

Additional directions beyond the first sheet. Top row: alternative ways to draw the rolling-7 trend (instead of 7 bars). Bottom row: alternative ways to surface the breach in the header (instead of a banner). All mocked at panel scale — nothing changed in the app.

Rolling-7 trend — alternative forms

Option D · Line + shaded deficit
Rolling 7-day rest
Trending 5h short of the 77h minimum.

Reads as a single trajectory dipping under the line — emphasises "it's been below all week and where it is now". Quietest of all; loses the per-day legibility of bars.
