Cargo → PYA Autofill

Fill your PYA Sea Service Testimonial (SST) in seconds.

Prepare a crew member's sea-service record in Cargo, click "Copy for PYA", then
open the PYA "Verify Sea Service Testimonial" page and click "Fill from Cargo".
The extension fills the vessel details, dates, sea-service day totals, capacity,
areas cruised, flag and signatory email straight into the form — no re-typing.

• One click to fill — the values come from the record you already prepared in Cargo.
• Fills the tricky bits too: the sail/motor and capacity radios, the areas-cruised
  checkboxes, and the country-flag picker.
• Nothing is submitted automatically — you always review before you submit.
• Private by design: it reads only the record you copied, only when you click Fill,
  and sends nothing anywhere. It runs only on member.pya.org.

For yacht crew and command using Cargo to track and export sea time.
